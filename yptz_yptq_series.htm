 YPTZ, YPTQ 3-PHASE AC FREQUENCY-CONTROL VARIABLE-SPEED ASYNCHRONOUS MOTOR
 

Main Features:

1. Compact in construction, small in volume and light weight

2. High running efficiency

3. Frame and end cover are completed by precisely casting workmanship with high-grade cast iron. Well designed and excellent machining workmanship make these motors have good heat radiation. The frame and end cover obtain high strength and rigidity

4. Integrated stator iron-pack is forcedly fitted into the frame with good quality assembly and convenient for maintenance

5. The insulation reliability of windings is improved because they are to be treated by VPI
