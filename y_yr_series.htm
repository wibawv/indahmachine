 Y, YR SERIES MED SIZED HIGH VOLTAGE 6KV 3-PHASE ASYNCHRONOUS MOTOR
 

Y, YR Series medium-sized high voltage 3-phase asynchronous motors are up-to-date products to replace the series JS, JR 3 phase asynchronous motors. Comparing with the old series, Y, YR new series motors are compact in volume, high in efficiency, reliable in operation and easy to mount and maintain. The new series motors are also advanced in mechanical, electrical and insulation performance and high technology in manufacturing.

Y, YR series motors are used to drive compressor, ventilation, water pump, breaker, cutting machine, grinding mill and other general purpose machines.

The rated frequency of Y, YR series motor is 50 HZ. Class F insulation is adopted for the motor and the rotating direction of the motor is clockwise viewed from the shaft extension. Also the motor can be made in counter-clockwise according to user’s requirement.
