 YJS Series Compact 3-phase Asynchronous Motor

GENERAL FEATURES:

Series YJS compact 3-phase cage induction motor is developed by LEC with experience of producing series Y3-phase cage induction motor. It is the latest generation of the series, which is wider in application range, higher in protection degree, better in technical performance, compact in construction, and better in saving energy and can completely meet the demands of the environmental protection. This series of products have been developed since 1992 and it was approved by the “State-Grade New Product” in 1998. Thanks to 6 years of hard work of many of our employees, the product with perfectly design, excellent workmanship, high grade mechanical energy, good reliability, small noise, low vibration, and convenience mounting and maintenance.

The product is widely known and appreciated by customers locally and abroad. The insulation class is F and supply voltage needed is 380v, 6kv, and 10kv. It's installation dimensions and power are copletely in conformity with the standard IEC72.
